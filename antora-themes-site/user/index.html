Getting Started

Welcome to the Antora Themes Gallery! This guide will help you prepare and submit your Antora UI theme.

What is the Antora Themes Gallery?

The gallery is a central directory of community-created Antora documentation themes. Theme developers can:

  • Showcase their themes to the Antora community

  • Get discovered by documentation teams looking for themes

  • Share their work without hosting infrastructure

How It Works

The gallery uses a decentralized model:

  1. You host your theme’s preview on GitHub Pages (free)

  2. You add a screenshot to your repository

  3. You submit your repository URL to the gallery

  4. The gallery displays your theme with links to your demo

You maintain full control of your theme repository. The gallery only stores metadata and links - it doesn’t copy or rebuild your theme.

Quick Start

  1. Check the requirements

  2. Use our template or add to existing repo

  3. Submit your theme
